The Photographer

Born in Chicago, I traded the concrete jungle for the mountains when I moved to Utah in 2015. There I developed a deep appreciation for the stillness of wild landscapes, public land, and a love for outdoor recreation including rock climbing and ski mountaineering. My path in the Earth Sciences has carried me around the world, camera never far from reach. Through both film and digital photography, I aim to create images that inspire others to step outside and pursue their own adventure.

From the high mountains of British Columbia to the far edges of the Greenland Sea. Eight years with a camera, too many travel miles to count. My work has appeared in Rolling Stone Magazine, Spotify, and a number of photo lab marketing campaigns.
